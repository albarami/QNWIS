2. **Second-Order Effects:**
   - Focusing on logistics could attract more international companies, creating jobs and boosting the economy. It could also enhance Qatar's trade volumes and re-export capabilities, making it a critical link in global supply chains.

3. **Relative Attractiveness of Options:**
   - Qatar's strategic geographic position between Europe, Asia, and Africa gives it a unique advantage in logistics. Hamad Airport is already close to Dubai's cargo capacity, so expanding it could make Qatar a key player in the region's logistics and trade.

4. **Key Risks and Opportunities:**
   - Focusing on logistics offers a more feasible path to becoming a regional leader, especially given the current capacity and proximity to key markets. This aligns with Qatar's Vision 2030 targets, such as increasing the knowledge economy share and improving female labor participation.
