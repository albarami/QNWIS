1. **Health & Healthcare**
   - Healthcare infrastructure investment
   - Medical tourism strategy
   - Public health metrics

   - Agricultural sector development
   - Supply chain resilience

4. **Skills Development & Education**
   - Human capital development
   - Technical training programs
   - Educational outcomes
   - Competitiveness rankings

5. **Digital Transformation**

6. **Oil & Gas / Energy**

15. **ANYTHING else the economic committee discusses**

## Why 15+ APIs?

The diverse API integration enables cross-domain analysis:

- **World Bank:** All sector GDP data (not just labor)
- **IMF:** Full economic dashboard (inflation, debt, growth)
- **IEA:** Energy sector (oil, gas, renewables)
- **UNWTO:** Tourism statistics
- **FAO:** Food and agriculture
- **ILO:** Labor markets
- **UNCTAD:** Investment climate
- **UN Comtrade:** Trade data
- **FRED:** Economic benchmarks
- **GCC-STAT:** Regional comparisons (ALL sectors)
- **Semantic Scholar:** Research on ANY topic
- **Brave:** News on ANY topic
- **Perplexity:** Real-time analysis on ANY topic
- **MoL LMIS:** Qatar labor data
- **Qatar Open Data:** ALL government datasets

**This is a general-purpose ministerial intelligence platform, not a narrow labor/food system.**

## Multi-Agent Debate Across All Domains

The 4 specialist agents provide perspectives across ALL domains:

1. **Financial Economist** - Financial viability (ANY sector)
2. **Market Analyst** - Market dynamics (ANY industry)
3. **Operations Expert** - Implementation feasibility (ANY initiative)
4. **Research Scientist** - Evidence base (ANY topic)

**Example:** "Should Qatar invest QAR 20B in AI sector?"
- Financial: ROI analysis, fiscal impact
- Market: Global AI trends, GCC competition
- Operations: Implementation timeline, talent availability
- Research: 10 Semantic Scholar papers on AI economic impact

**The system debates ANY strategic decision, not just labor/food.**
